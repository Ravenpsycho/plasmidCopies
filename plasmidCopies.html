Calculate the number of copies in your sample using formula:

\(N \approx \frac{W * N_A}{L * 650 * 1E9}\)

Where:

  • \(N\) = number of copies
  • \(W\) = Weight in ng
  • \(N_A\) = Avogadro's Constant
  • \(L\) = Length in basepairs"
  • \(650\) = Molar weight of one basepair in grams
  • \(1E9\) = grams to nanogramms conversion
